Events

LA VIBES was created on the idea that music is one of the things in this world that unites people from all parts of the world. With all of the current events going on in the world, Los Angeles is one of the few cities where you can achieve the American dream, Regardless of your background of heritage. You could succeed if you really put your mind to it. Los Angeles “The City of Angels” lives up to its name, a city of hope. For our events we like to promote local artists as well as up and coming artists. There is so much talent that goes unnoticed, we would like to change. We would like to help as many people as possible so if you would like to give music a try give us a shout! LA VIBES is also committed to helping those in need. Each one of our events has a charity set up to help out others and raise awareness of those less fortunate. Our goal from this is to promote unity through music as well as helping others and doing it with a smile on our face. WE ARE LA VIBES.
